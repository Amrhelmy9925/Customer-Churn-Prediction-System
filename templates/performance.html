Metric Guide

ROC AUC — Measures the model's ability to distinguish between churners and non-churners. Values closer to 1.0 are better.

Accuracy — Overall proportion of correct predictions out of all predictions.

Precision — Of all customers predicted to churn, how many actually did. Important when false positives are costly.

Recall — Of all customers who actually churned, how many were correctly identified. Important when missing churners is costly.

F1 Score — Harmonic mean of precision and recall. Balances both concerns.

Log Loss — Penalizes confident but wrong predictions. Lower is better.
